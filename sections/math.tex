Compression ratio:
$$CR=\frac{m*n}{r(m+n+1)}$$

Mean square error:
$$MSE=\sum \frac{(O_{ij} - R_{ij})^{2}}{mn}$$,
where O represents the original image and R represents the
reconstructed image of dimension mxn.

Peak signal to noise ratio:
$$PSNR=10\log({255}^{2}MSE)$$
